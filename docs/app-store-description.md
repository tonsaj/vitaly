## Description - English

**Vitaly - Your Personal Health Dashboard**

Take control of your health with Vitaly, the beautifully designed app that transforms your Apple Health data into actionable insights.

**UNDERSTAND YOUR BODY**
• Recovery Score - Know when to push hard and when to rest
• Strain Tracking - Monitor your daily physical load
• Sleep Analysis - Deep dive into your sleep stages and quality
• Heart Metrics - Track HRV, resting heart rate, and trends

**AI-POWERED INSIGHTS**
Let our intelligent assistant analyze your health patterns and provide personalized recommendations. Get daily coaching tips based on your unique data.

**BEAUTIFUL DASHBOARD**
All your key health metrics at a glance. Our WHOOP-inspired design makes complex health data easy to understand.

**WHAT WE TRACK**
• Sleep duration and stages (Deep, REM, Core)
• Heart rate and heart rate variability
• Steps and active calories
• Recovery and strain scores
• Body measurements

**GLP-1 MEDICATION TRACKER (Beta)**
For those on weight loss medications like Ozempic, Wegovy, or Mounjaro:
• Track injections and dosages
• Monitor side effects
• Visualize weight loss progress
• Get dose escalation reminders

**PRIVACY FIRST**
Your health data stays yours. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. All data is encrypted and securely stored.

**REQUIREMENTS**
• iPhone with iOS 17.0 or later
• Apple Watch recommended for best experience
• HealthKit enabled

Download Vitaly today and start your journey to better health.
