Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect Vitaly to Apple Health?

When you first open Vitaly, you'll be asked to grant permission to access your health data. You can also enable this later in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Vitaly.

Why isn't my sleep data showing?

Sleep data requires an Apple Watch or a compatible sleep tracking app that writes to Apple Health. Make sure your device is recording sleep and syncing to Apple Health.

How is my Recovery Score calculated?

Your Recovery Score is based on several factors including sleep quality, heart rate variability (HRV), resting heart rate, and sleep duration. A higher score means your body is well-recovered.

Is my health data secure?

Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Firebase's enterprise-grade security. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the Vitaly app and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data from our servers within 30 days.

What is the GLP-1 Tracker?

The GLP-1 Tracker is a beta feature for people taking weight loss medications like Ozempic, Wegovy, or Mounjaro. It helps you track injections, monitor side effects, and visualize your progress.

How do I enable injection reminders?

In the GLP-1 Tracker, tap "Edit Treatment" and enable "Injection Reminder". You can set your preferred day and time for notifications.
