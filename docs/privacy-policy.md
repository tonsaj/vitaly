# Privacy Policy for Vitaly

**Last updated: January 15, 2026**

## Introduction

Vitaly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS health tracking application.

## Information We Collect

### Health Data (via Apple HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, Vitaly accesses the following health data from Apple HealthKit:

- **Sleep Data**: Sleep duration, sleep stages (deep, REM, core)
- **Heart Data**: Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV)
- **Activity Data**: Steps, active energy burned, exercise minutes
- **Body Measurements**: Weight, height, body mass index

**Important**: We only read health data from HealthKit. We never write or modify your HealthKit data without your explicit action.

### Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Date of birth (optional, for personalized insights)
- Height (optional, for BMI calculations)

### GLP-1 Medication Tracking (Optional)

If you use our GLP-1 tracker feature, you may optionally provide:
- Medication type and dosage
- Injection logs
- Side effect reports
- Body composition measurements

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Display Health Metrics**: Show your health data in an easy-to-understand dashboard
2. **Generate AI Insights**: Analyze patterns in your health data to provide personalized recommendations
3. **Track Progress**: Help you monitor your health goals over time
4. **Medication Reminders**: Send notifications for medication schedules (if enabled)

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

- **Health Data**: Processed locally on your device and synced to Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud) with encryption
- **Account Data**: Stored securely in Firebase Authentication and Firestore
- **AI Processing**: Health summaries are sent to Google's Gemini AI for analysis (no raw HealthKit data is shared)

### Security Measures

- All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption
- Firebase provides enterprise-grade security
- We never store your Apple ID or HealthKit credentials

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your personal or health data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your health data for marketing purposes

**We may share data with:**
- **Google Firebase**: For secure data storage and authentication
- **Google Gemini AI**: Aggregated health summaries for generating insights (no personally identifiable information)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You can:
- View all your stored data within the app
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings
- Disable notifications at any time

### Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Go to Settings in the Vitaly app
2. Tap "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

This will permanently remove all your data from our servers within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

Vitaly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

- **Email**: privacy@vitaly.app
- **Website**: https://vitaly.app/privacy

## Apple HealthKit Compliance

This app complies with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:
- Health data is only used to provide health and fitness services to the user
- Health data is never used for advertising or data mining
- Health data is never shared with third parties without explicit user consent
- Health data is never sold to any party
